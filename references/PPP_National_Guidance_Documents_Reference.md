# EU Member State National & Zonal Guidance Documents - PPP Registration & Risk Assessment

**Scope:** National guidance, zonal working documents, and national evaluation frameworks from EU member state competent authorities, covering PPP authorisation under Regulation (EC) No 1107/2009.

**Last compiled:** May 2026. Documents in this category are frequently updated; always check the issuing authority's website for the current version.

> **Zonal membership under Reg 1107/2009:**
> - **Northern Zone (NZ):** DK, EE, FI, LT, LV, SE (+ NO as EEA participant)
> - **Central Zone (CZ):** AT, BE, CZ, DE, FR, HU, IE, LU, NL, PL, RO, SK, SI
> - **Southern Zone (SZ):** BG, CY, ES, EL (GR), HR, IT, MT, PT
>
> Interzonal uses (greenhouse, post-harvest, empty storage rooms, seed treatments) are assessed by a single MS on behalf of the entire EU.
>
> Documents hosted on **CIRCABC** (the EU Commission collaboration platform) are generally accessible to registered stakeholders; some are also published publicly by individual MS authorities.

## 5. Netherlands - Ctgb (Board for the Authorisation of Plant Protection Products and Biocides)

**Website:** https://english.ctgb.nl

## 14. UK (Great Britain and Northern Ireland) - HSE CRD
