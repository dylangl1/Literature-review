# PPP Registration & Risk Assessment: Guidance Document Reference

**Scope:** SANCO/SANTE (EC Commission), FOCUS, EFSA, and EPPO guidance documents relevant to EU plant protection product (PPP) registration and risk assessment under Regulation (EC) No 1107/2009. National guidance documents relevant to EU PPP registration is outlined in can be found in "/PPP_National_Guidance_Documents_Reference.md". OECD Test Guidelines can be found in the 'assets/oecd-test-guidelines' folder.

**Last compiled:** May 2026. Always verify current version before use - documents are revised periodically.

> Documents marked *(superseded)* remain in use in certain contexts (e.g. national authorisations, older dossiers) but have been replaced at EU AS approval level. Documents marked *(noted by SCoPAFF)* have been formally acknowledged by the Standing Committee on Plants, Animals, Food and Feed.

---

## 1. Legislative Framework

These are the core regulations underpinning the guidance documents below.

| Instrument | Title | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Regulation (EC) No 1107/2009 | Placing of plant protection products on the market (repealing Directives 79/117/EEC and 91/414/EEC) | Primary legislation |
| Regulation (EU) No 283/2013 | Data requirements for active substances | Annex to 1107/2009 |
| Regulation (EU) No 284/2013 | Data requirements for plant protection products | Annex to 1107/2009 |
| Regulation (EU) No 546/2011 | Uniform principles for evaluation and authorisation of PPPs | Implementing regulation |
| Regulation (EU) No 2020/1740 | Provisions for the renewal procedure (replacing Reg 844/2012) | Renewal regulation |
| Regulation (EU) No 2021/428 | Standard data formats for submission of applications (IUCLID) | Format regulation |
| Regulation (EC) No 396/2005 | Maximum residue levels of pesticides in food and feed | MRL regulation |
| Regulation (EU) No 2018/605 | Amending 546/2011 - scientific criteria for endocrine disruptors | ED criteria |

## 5. EPPO Standards

EPPO standards are maintained at https://www.eppo.int/RESOURCES/eppo_standards

### 5.1 PP 1 - Efficacy Evaluation of Plant Protection Products

PP 1 standards are the primary reference for efficacy data generation and reporting. General standards are freely accessible; crop/pest-specific standards require an EPPO subscription.

### 5.2 PP 3 - Environmental Risk Assessment of Plant Protection Products

> **Note:** The entire PP 3 series was **withdrawn in 2018**. These documents are no longer maintained by EPPO. The environmental risk assessment framework has transitioned fully to EFSA and SANCO/SANTE guidance under Reg 1107/2009. They are listed here for historical reference only.

Former PP 3 standards covered topics including: risk assessment for soil organisms, non-target arthropods, non-target plants, aquatic organisms, birds and mammals, and groundwater. These were largely superseded during the transition from Directive 91/414/EEC to Regulation 1107/2009.
